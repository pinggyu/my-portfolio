About

Hi, I'm Ping ⎯ a web developer based in Toronto, Canada.

I'm passionate about building responsive and accessible web applications in order to create great user experiences. Previously, I was working at a large consulting firm in financial services as a CPA and in tech advisory. I also have experience working in bizops for an early stage tech startup. I am very adaptable and love learning new technologies and frameworks.
